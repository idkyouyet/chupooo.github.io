CHAPTER 1
It was spring. Breeze was sweet and full of fragranced petals that wrapped around her. She was walking by the street to her favourite coffee place. Everything around her felt warm. She sat at her usual place and continued to write the story for her next book.
Suddenly the door opens. Air gushes in. The tiny bell hung above it rings. She hears him before she sees him. He was on a call and as soon as he entered the coffee place his call jst ended. He walks up to the barista.
Tony-"Hi Emma..."
Emma-"You are looking good Tony."
Tony-"Thanks......You are the best."
Emma-"There you go."
He gives a tender smile to her and takes his iced cappuccino and sits on the other side of the place facing towards me. In this short while I noticed him and it felt like time had stopped. I noticed his sweet gestures. He was wearing a black jacket and white tshirt inside it with blue jeans. He had big brown eyes and long dark lashes and black messed up hair - he had it all. He looked cute. His dressing sense was all describing his personality.


CHAPTER 2
Today is a beautiful day. The sun is shining brightly, the grass is greener than ever, and the skies are pure blue. All around me is at peace. I have always wanted a day off. What could be much better than having a cappuccino in a vintage place ?
It feels good to be here. My eyes scanned the whole place and the place was quite empty. I see a women sitting right infront of me. My eyes got stuck on her. I started wondering...
Who is she ?
Have I ever seen her ?
I noticed her for a long time. She was fair and had dark brown hair that covered her bare shoulder and her face and I could still see the sunlight reaching her cheeks as if it wanted to desperately kiss her. She was wearing a white sleeveless dress with heels that exactly matched to her skin colour. She had huge eyes. Brown ones that sparkled amber. Oh god!! I couldn't stop staring at her eyes. All this while i noticed her. I fell in love with her the moment I saw her but who does that ?
I sat there for hours and couldn't stop thinking about her. I watched her continuously typing something in her laptop and sipping coffee and putting her hair behind her ears. Her small actions made me fall deeper.
Should i go to her ?
Random thoughts in my mind. Nope she is just a girl in a coffee shop...
But i couldn't stop myself so i go to her.

CHAPTER 6
I take her to my car. I open the door for her. I talk even while driving. I wasn't able to stop talking to her and nor was she. We both seemed really invested into each other. It took me just one unplanned date to fall in love with her. I am still not sure about this. But i like were this thing was heading out.
She took me to her favourite place. It was a park. She lies down and seeing her so do I.
Pepper-"I love to come here during the night. This place becomes quite. I come here, lay on the grass, play my favourite music and just watch the stars. Their sparkle fascinates me. They make me wonder about the meaning and purpose of human existence, wonder about our tiny size in the infinite universe"
I kept listening to her speak and I understood what she exactly meant. I loved to listen to her voice as if her voice was some beautiful music. I watched her sparkling eyes under the dark sky and it reminded me that yes maybe this is it. That moment I knew that I was in love with her and she was still some girl I met in the coffee shop.
